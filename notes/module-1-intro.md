# Module 1: AI Foundations & Evolution

## Key Concepts
### 1. AI vs. Augmented Intelligence
- **Artificial Intelligence (AI):** A broad field aiming to create systems that can perform tasks requiring human-like intelligence.
- **Augmented Intelligence:** A subsection of AI that focuses on **enhancing** human capabilities rather than replacing them. IBM emphasizes this "human-in-the-loop" approach.

### 2. Traditional AI vs. Generative AI
- **Traditional (Discriminative) AI:** Good at classifying data (e.g., "Is this email spam or not?"). It follows fixed rules and patterns.
- **Generative AI:** Powered by Foundation Models. It creates **new** content (text, images, code) based on the data it was trained on.
